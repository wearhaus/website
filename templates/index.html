LISTENING FEATURES
WIRELESS SYNCING
The Arc is the world’s first pair of headphones that lets you wirelessly sync up with multiple friends and listen together. Syn- chronized listening is only a touch away.
TOUCH CONTROLS
No more fumbling with your phone to change a song -- the Arc uses touch controls on the right earcup to skip between songs, adjust volume, broadcast your audio or join a station.
WIRED LISTENING
Battery running low? Need to use headphones with your DJ setup? The Arc comes with a gold-plated 3.5mm AUX cable so you can listen from any wired audio source.
